Pasta


  • Time:15 min.
  • Serves:2 pers.
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato sauce 250 mL
Fresh pasta 250 gr.
Mince meat 100 gr.

Step 1: Pour the minced meat and the tomato sauce at the pan with slow cooking.
Step 2: Puth the fresh pasta in a cooking pot with boiling water; 8 min, until the pasta is soft.
Step 3: Pour the pasta on the pan with everything and mix.
Step 4: ¡enjoy!

Endive salad

  • Time:10 min
  • Serves:3 pers.
Ingredients Quantity
Endive 2 buds
Nuts 100 gr.
Apple 1 ud
Cheese 100gr

Step 1:Separe the buds of endive and cut the apple and the cheese in cubes.
Step 2:Pour everything on a bowl
Step 3:Season with some lemon, salt and pepper
Step 4: ¡Enjoy!
